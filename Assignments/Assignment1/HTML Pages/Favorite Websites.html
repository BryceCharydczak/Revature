Great Resources for safe and legal air-travel

Transportation Security Administration is the most important link here because these are the people that you will have direct contact with prior to flying.
TSA is responsible for making sure that every person is transporting only legal and ethically sound materials and devices through airport terminals.

The Department of Homeland Security is an excellent resource for understanding legal items for safe air travel.
This website has a frequently asked questions section that will enable you to answer some questions you may have prior to travel.

Wikipedia is a good resource for understanding what will be conducted during your time in the airport.
Wikipedia does an excellent job of highlighting the different technologies that are present in a airport.
